Online Resources

There is an abundance of health information that can be found on the internet, and it is difficult to determine what is truly credible and reliable information.
This collection of links is made up of credible, respected health-related sites that will help you in your information search.
This information is not meant to replace a consultation with a physician.
Please print and bring along any information you wish to discuss to your next appointment.
