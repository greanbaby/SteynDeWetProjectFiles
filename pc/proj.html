Project Goals:

Give healthcare providers easy ways to view patient health information on any device they choose--phone, tablet, laptop, or large desktop screen.

Provide patients with access to their health information to help them partner in their healthcare.

Short Term:

No Short Term items.

Medium Term:

Customize patient help.
e.g. a value of 6.8% for HbA1c shows different help than a 13.2%.

Long Term:

Display other Screening items they are due for at the top (copy what is now showing to LPN staff on weekly list if anything).

Any test out of normal range will generate extra text box to alert that this result needs attention.

Add a statistics history page to enable viewing of each previous weeks or months changes over time.

Change color of patient cards depending on Framingham risk, e.g. a red one means risk of heart attack in next 10 years is 33%.

Add a link to converter so people can relate their test result numbers to American doctors that use different measurement units.

Secure pages so each patient can only view their own (Wolf Portal will provide this username/password?).

Display clinic data for patient comparison.

Add checkboxes to indicate on Statin? and on ACE/ARB?

Add photo of patient.
